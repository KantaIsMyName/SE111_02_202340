The Fundamentals of Caring

"Caring is a funny thing"

Meet Ben Benjamin, a recently trained caretaker of special needs individuals who's been down on himself. His wife is trying to push for divorce, even though they haven't been together for two years. His job goes down the drain. And his world grows increasingly bleaker by the day. But things change when Ben meets his new patient 'Trevor'. A sardonic, overly sarcastic, and lightly incindiery individual who longs for some sort of release from his mundanity. Hence the duo take a roadtrip around the united states as Trevor loves American road side attractions. So pack your bag and hop in the van, we're going cross country!
